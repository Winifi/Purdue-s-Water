Purdue's Water

The Problem

Purdue constantly denies the problems with the water, but students and faculty disagree. The Consumer Quality report says that Purdue is definitely not in violation of any levels of toxicities, but students are not satisfied with the quality of the drinking water. Despite Purdue having installed drinking fountains with water filters in them, these are often not up kept and the filters are mainly red, signaling that they need to be changed.
